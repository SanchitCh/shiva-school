About School

At SHIVA we believe that schooling is an experience. To achieve that students are provided a completely balanced environment focused on Shaping children into tomorrow's leaders. We deem that each child follows the feet of a different drummer hence we encourage our students to be original. Students from SHIVA, look forward to a prospective future in which each of them feel gratified of themselves.

Reinforcement Classes

MISSION:Students securing 50% and below are put in remedial classes. These classes are organized for those children who require extra coaching. Intensive and exam oriented coaching is offered to ensure enhanced performance.

Student life: SHIVA facilitates students with an array of co-curricular and extracurricular activities from sports to community service complements academic program.

FACILITIES

Transport Facility

The school provides transportation for the convenience and the safety of students. The school operates its own buses on specific routes, depending on the number of students on the route. Though we try our best, it may not always be possible to drop students at their doorstep or to accommodate requests for changes. Once a route has been finalised no changes/diversions will be entertained, and parents are requested to co-operate. If any student is found violating the code of conduct for the bus, the bus facility may be withdrawn. The onus of deciding the gravity of the situation is on the teacher in charge of the bus.

Computer Labs

We have created an extensive computer lab with provision to accommodate each student with a computer during the class. All computers are loaded with latest software and educational material to provide comprehensive digital learning. Computer education initiates young students into the fascinating world of computing and introduces them to its broad and ever growing choice of application. Our lab is arranged in a well furnished spacious room with sufficient tables, chairs and other equipments. All computers are new and updated with facilities like internet with broadband connection.
